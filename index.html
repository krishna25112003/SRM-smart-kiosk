Overview

SRM is among India’s earliest large multidisciplinary higher educational institutions that now caters 50000+ students across 6 campuses. SRM’s Directorate of Admissions manages admissions for students from across India as well as for International students from all around the world. SRM is often seen as a Mini-India. SRM is an ideal destination for those aspiring to pursue under-graduate, post-graduate, PhD or advanced studies. The promise of an enriching student life experience in a vastly multi-cultural, green and high ranked institution are but few reasons you must consider SRM for your higher education. We welcome you to take a look at our Admissions section of this website. This will serve you as a gateway to understanding about the various programs offered, eligibility criteria, fees, scholarships and loan assistance. It enables you to have an easy going application and admissions process. You most certainly must reach out to us in case of any questions.
